Achievements

At age 18, during her first semester of college, Emily was accepted to her first ever professional, juried art gallery. Her two polaroid pieces Leah and Monson were both done in 2020. This kickstarted her career as a professional arist.

What has she done since?

Emily started her art career with high realism, but quickly found it boring. She decided to change up her work and focus of drawings and paintings that make people smile.
